Mi-Care App

Individual project for Msc degree in Visual Communication and Interface Design, 2021

Mission and process

My mission was to redesign Mi-Care, an application to predict and manage psychotic onset. To accomplish this goal I followed the typical design process. Here we have some of these steps.

  1. I studied the state of art and the app that has been already designed.
  2. Then I analyzed the goals and the new functions I could have introduced. These new features are the tracing of emotions, working/school activities and cognitive abilities, a chatbot with an avatar therapist, and three digital diaries.
  3. I did a benchmark to take inspiration for the app UI.
  4. I carried out UX research, interviewing two therapists and studying the psychotic onsets.
  5. Then, I built proto-personas.
  6. I made wireframes for the new UI app.
  7. I designed and prototyped the UI of Mi-Care app, using Figma.
  8. Finally, I prepared the future user tests, that the group of researchers will bring out in the next period.
