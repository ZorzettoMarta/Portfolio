arTech Blog

Group project exam Web Tools and Application, 2020

Our Mission and results

Our mission was to create a blog using WordPress. We had to change the blog style thanks to CSS and to make some editorial content. The meeting between the world of art and the one of technology was our topic.

To support our blog we created and managed the related social media pages on Instagram, Facebook and Twitter.

Eventually, we monitored the blog trend thanks to Google Analytics to try to understand its impact on the public.

Information architecture

Blog Concept

The arTech blog wants to explain to the public how the union of art and technology can create innovation in this field.

From the digitalization of artworks to the possibility to make accessible exhibitions and events, the combination of these two worlds can express the best of both, enriching one another and developing new opportunities.
