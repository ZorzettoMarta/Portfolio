Taj Mahal: an heritage rich in love

Individual project exam for Visual Communication Laboratory, 2020

Project brief and process

My goal was to create a website to promote a Unesco heritage starting from zero.
I designed it following the typical design process and I developed it using only html and css.

  1. I made a mood board to understand which would has been the website mood and the concept I would have like to bring forward.
  2. Then I made some stylistic choices: the palette, the typeface and the type of icons to use.
  3. I rapidly prototyped the different web pages of the site by hand.
  4. I realized the entire mockup of the website in Photoshop.
  5. Eventually, I developed the website, using only HTML5 and CSS.

Here you can see some of the steps I carried out to design the Taj Mahal site.

Logo

I created the logo getting inspired by the unique Taj Mahal’s shape. I wanted to lead to the extreme forms to have a minimal and essential logo. We can see the big central dome of the mausoleum, the two smaller beside and the two minarets that are in the garden around the monument.
To create the logo I used Adobe Illustrator and I exported it in SVG format not to lose the quality of it.
