Dress4U: the smart wardrobe

Group project exam Ubiquitous, Pervasive & Context-aware Computing, 2021

Mission and process

Our challenge was to invent a new device that had to be blend in the enviroment and the user had to use it with less effort possible. This technology had to be both persuasive and pervasive.
To create Dress4U we did the following steps.

  1. We studied the state of art of other smart device on the market.
  2. To understand our target and the possible features of Dress4U, we administered a survey.
  3. We built the personas and for each one a related scenario.
  4. We designed the structure of the wardrobe, using SketchUp.
  5. We defined all the smart features of Dress4U and of the related app.
  6. We made the mockup of the UI both of the wardrobe and app, using Figma.

The wardrobe and its UI

Here beside you can appriciate the 3D render of Dress4U. First of all we drew the wardrobe by hand, then we used SketchUp to create the prototype. Instead, to design the UI of the closet door we used Figma. Click on the wardrobe to navigate the prototype of Dress4U!

The Dress4U App

The Dress 4U App is an app created to support the smart wardrobe features and to control it from distance.
From your phone:

  1. you can consult your agenda that is synchronized with the smartphone calendar;
  2. you can see inside the wardrobe thanks to the cameras placed in the doors and in the shelves of the closet;
  3. you can search for a specific piece of clothing;
  4. you can add and categorize a new piece of clothing.
