Hi! I'm Marta Zorzetto and I'm currently working as a UX-UI designer in the healthcare field in Milan.
My interests regard the product design world, in particular user experience design and user interface design. As a designer with a computer science background, I have a unique point of view on the design of digital solutions and thanks to my programming skills I'm a quick learner when it comes to getting familiar with new software.
I'm a very determined person with a strong team spirit, ready to step outside my comfort zone and grow in the professional world.
I not only work, but I also have many other interests. I love cooking, doing yoga, and reading. Moreover, I'm fond of photography, interior design, and art. At least, I'm a backpacker, always ready to discover the world.
